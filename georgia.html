Georgia Runoff

Control of Senate

2020 Senate elections were held on Tuesday November 3rd 2020. After the election, Republican held 50 seats, Democratic held 46 seats, 2 Independents who both caucused with the Democratic party, and 2 undecided seats both from Georgia. Due to Georgia law, none of the candidates got the majority of the votes in both seats, triggering a runoff for top 2 candidates each seats on Tuesday January 5th 2021. One seat has incumbent Republican David Perdue with Democratic challenger Jon Ossoff advanced to a runoff, while other seat has incumbent Republican Kelly Loeffler with Democratic challenger Raphael Warnock advanced to a runoff.
